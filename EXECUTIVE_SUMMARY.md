# CarbonOpus - Executive Summary
Empowering Independent Artists. Rewarding Fans. Revolutionizing Music.

## THE OPPORTUNITY
The music industry extracts 70-85% of independent artist revenue through middlemen while 12M+ artists worldwide struggle to monetize their work. Streaming pays ~$0.003 per stream—artists need 300K+ streams monthly just to earn minimum wage. Meanwhile, 100M+ music fans have no way to financially participate in the success of artists they discover and champion.
We're building the platform that changes everything.

## THE SOLUTION
A social-first music platform combining direct sales, fan reward mechanics, AI-powered tools, and creator tokenization—enabling artists to keep 84-99% of revenue while fans profit from sharing music they love.

### Core Value Propositions:

**For Artists:**
* Keep 84-99% of sales vs. 15-30% on streaming platforms
* Set your own prices and control your economics
* Launch creator tokens—let fans invest in your success
* AI-powered content creation and cross-platform posting
* Own direct relationships with your fans

**For Fans:**
* Earn money by sharing music (15% resale rewards set by artists)
* True ownership of purchased music
* Invest in artists through creator tokens
* Participate financially in artist success

**How It Works:** 
Artist uploads → Fan buys → Fan shares → New fan buys → Original fan earns 15% → Artist earns 84% → Platform takes 1%
Result: Viral growth loop where everyone profits and great music spreads organically.
