# Gemini Code Assistant: CarbonOpus Docs Context

This file provides the core context and instructions for the Gemini Code Assistant when working within the CarbonOpus repository.

## Project Overview
CarbonOpus is a social-first music platform that empowers independent artists and rewards fans. It challenges the traditional streaming model by offering direct sales, fan-driven resale rewards, creator tokens, and AI-powered content tools.

## Core Domain Mechanics
When writing documentation or features, ensure alignment with these core platform mechanics:
*   **Artist Revenue Share:** Artists keep **84-99%** of their music sales (disrupting the ~$0.003/stream model).
*   **Fan Resale Rewards:** Fans earn a percentage (default **15%**) when they successfully share/refer music to others. This drives viral growth.
*   **True Ownership:** Fans truly own the digital music they purchase directly from artists.
*   **Creator Tokens:** Artists can launch custom coins (with a 1% launch fee) to let fans invest in their success.
*   **Platform Fee:** CarbonOpus takes a flat 1% fee on music sales.
*   **AI Tools:** Artists have access to AI personas and content generation to automate their social media marketing.

## Assistant Instructions & Conventions
1.  **Tone & Style:** Maintain a professional, empowering, and revolutionary tone. Emphasize "direct ownership," "creator empowerment," and the "viral growth loop" where both artists and fans profit.

    *   Ensure guides are user-centric, clearly separating the "Artist" and "Fan" experiences.
    *   Format content to be easily scannable with bullet points and bold text for key metrics (like 84-99% or 15%).
3.  **Reference Material:**
