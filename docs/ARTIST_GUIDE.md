# CarbonOpus Artist Guide

Welcome to CarbonOpus! This guide will help you get started as an artist on our platform, where you take control of your music, your audience, and your revenue.

## Why CarbonOpus?
The traditional music industry often extracts 70-85% of independent artist revenue. On major streaming platforms, you might earn as little as **$0.003 per stream**, requiring hundreds of thousands of streams just to earn a living wage. 

**CarbonOpus is building a platform that changes everything.** 

*   **Keep Your Earnings:** Keep **84-99%** of all your direct music sales.
*   **Viral Growth:** Leverage your fans as a marketing force through built-in resale rewards.
*   **Direct Ownership:** Own the direct relationship with your fans without middlemen.

## 1. Getting Started
*   **Connect Your Wallet:** Connect your Web3 wallet (e.g., MetaMask) or sign up with your email to create a managed wallet.
*   **Set Up Your Profile:** Add your artist name, bio, profile picture, and social links. A complete profile helps fans discover and trust you.

## 2. Uploading & Selling Music
*   **Upload Tracks:** Upload your high-quality audio files directly to the platform.
*   **Set Your Price:** You decide how much your music costs. No more waiting for fractions of a cent per stream.
*   **Resale Rewards:** When you upload music, you set a resale reward (default 15%). This incentivizes fans to share your music—when they successfully refer a new buyer, they earn a cut, and you keep the rest (minus a 1% platform fee).

## 3. Creator Tokens (Creator Coins)
Turn your success into a shared economy with your fans.
*   **Launch Your Token:** Launch your own unique Creator Token to let fans invest in your success.
*   **Align Incentives:** Fans who buy your token become early backers. As your career grows and platform activity increases, your token holders benefit alongside you.
*   **Token Launch:** Use our simple tools to launch your token with a 1% launch fee.

## 4. AI-Powered Content Tools
Spend less time marketing and more time creating.
*   **AI Characters:** Create an AI persona to interact with fans, answer questions, and stay active 24/7.
*   **Content Generation:** Use AI Credits to automatically generate high-quality social media posts, images, and marketing copy.
*   **Cross-Platform Posting:** Schedule and share your updates across all your connected social platforms with one click.

## 5. Analytics & Payouts
*   **Real-time Tracking:** Monitor profile views, sales, and fan engagement in your Artist Dashboard.
*   **Transparent Revenue:** See exactly where your earnings come from: Direct Sales, Fan Referrals, and Token activity.
*   **Instant Access:** Your on-chain earnings are available to you immediately. Claim them to your wallet whenever you like.
