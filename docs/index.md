# CarbonOpus Documentation

Welcome to the official documentation for **CarbonOpus**, the all-in-one social music platform empowering independent artists and rewarding fans.

## What is CarbonOpus?

CarbonOpus is a social-first music platform that revolutionizes how music is sold, shared, and supported. We are closing the gap in the music industry where middlemen extract the majority of revenue, leaving artists to struggle.

### Our Mission
To empower independent artists to take control of their careers and own their audience directly, while rewarding fans for discovering and championing the music they love.

### Core Value Propositions
*   **For Artists:** Keep 84-99% of your sales, set your own prices, launch creator tokens, and leverage AI-powered tools.
*   **For Fans:** True ownership of music, earn rewards for sharing tracks, and invest in your favorite artists' success.
