# CarbonOpus Fan Guide

Welcome to CarbonOpus! Discover new music, support your favorite independent artists directly, and get rewarded for being an active part of the community.

## Why CarbonOpus for Fans?
In the traditional streaming world, fans are just passive consumers. On CarbonOpus, you are a vital part of the artist's success and are rewarded for your support.

*   **True Ownership:** When you buy music on CarbonOpus, you own it. It's not just a license to stream—it's yours to keep.
*   **Earn as You Share:** Your taste in music is valuable. Help your favorite artists grow and earn a piece of the revenue you help generate.
*   **Invest in Success:** Buy Creator Tokens to financially participate in the success of the artists you believe in.

## 1. Getting Started
*   **Create an Account:** Connect an existing Web3 wallet or sign up with your email to create a managed account. No crypto experience required!
*   **Build Your Profile:** Customize your profile, follow your favorite artists, and start building your collection.

## 2. Discover & Buy Music
*   **Social Feed:** Browse your social feed to discover new tracks, see what your friends are listening to, and find trending artists.
*   **Support Artists Directly:** When you purchase a track, the vast majority of the money (up to 99%) goes directly to the artist.
*   **Digital Collection:** Every purchase is added to your permanent digital collection, accessible anytime, anywhere.

## 3. Earn by Sharing (Resale Rewards)
Turn your passion for music into a revenue stream.
*   **Get Your Link:** Every track you buy or discover comes with a unique referral link.
*   **Share the Music:** Share your favorite songs on social media, Discord, or directly with friends.
*   **Earn 15%:** When someone buys a track through your link, you earn a **15% reward** (as set by the artist).
*   **Viral Growth Loop:** Artist uploads → You buy → You share → New fan buys → You earn 15% → Artist earns 84% → Platform takes 1%. Everyone wins!

## 4. Invest in Artists (Creator Tokens)
Go beyond just listening—become an early backer and stakeholder.
*   **Buy Creator Tokens:** Purchase custom tokens launched by artists to show your long-term support.
*   **Early Access & Perks:** Holding tokens can unlock exclusive content, early access to releases, and other perks defined by the artist.
*   **Participate in Growth:** As the artist gains popularity and generates more activity on the platform, token holders benefit from being part of that ecosystem from the start.

## 5. Manage Your Portfolio
*   **Music Library:** Access all your purchased music and curated playlists.
*   **Earnings Dashboard:** Track the rewards you've earned from sharing music and referal activity.
*   **Token Portfolio:** Monitor the value and utility of the Creator Tokens you hold.
